Work Experience

Senior iOS Engineer - Reverb (Sep 2019 - Present)

iOS Engineer - Venmo (Jan 2019 - Sep 2019)

I developed new features for and maintained the Venmo iOS app. I integrated Bitmoji into the payment flow and increased UI test coverage. I was also responsible for mentoring more junior members of the team.

iOS Engineer - Sittercity (Sep 2016 - Dec 2018)

I developed new features for and maintained Sittercity and Chime iOS apps, connecting parents to babysitters and managing bookings and payments. I spearheaded development and integration of a native help center and chat framework into multiple apps. I also advocated for and implemented accessibility improvements to Chime app

iOS Engineer - Belly (Feb 2016 - Aug 2016)

I built a geofenced mobile check-ins feature allowing users to check in from their devices, with extensive in-field testing to develop accurate location heuristics. I also developed a Rails backend for a pilot loyalty program at major clothing retailer, built and integrated a Swift framework for UI elements to be shared within Belly iOS apps, and brought our member app up to modern standards of accessibility

iOS Engineer - Vokal Interactive (Sep 2014 - Dec 2015)

I was an iOS engineer contributing to client and internal projects at Vokal. I served as lead iOS developer on discovery and development for a dating app. I also helped to refactor and nativize a legacy e-commerce app for a Fortune 500 client.

iOS Apprentice - Detroit Labs (Mar 2014 - Jun 2014)

I graduated from Detroit Labs' inaugural apprentice program for iOS development. I contributed to a hybrid software-hardware client project in the auto industry, a social media app in App Store (Flipback), and a scavenger hunt app.
